About

What is PlanMyCourse?

PlanMyCourse is a decision aid for course planning. It's built on the idea that there is rarely one "best" schedule — only different trade-offs. Rather than optimizing for a single outcome, the tool helps you compare strategies and understand the consequences of your priorities across GPA, workload, progress, and enrollment risk.

The recommendations are generated from the information you provide and the constraints the system can check (such as prerequisites and time conflicts). Note that because course offerings and policies can change, PlanMyCourse is not a substitute for official enrollment tools.
